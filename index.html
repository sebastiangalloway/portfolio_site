About Me

   I am Sebastian Galloway, a Computer Science graduate from Florida International University and an IT Software Developer Associate at Progressive Insurance, where I build and support large-scale enterprise software systems that impact thousands of users. Since June 2025, I have contributed to SQL data pipeline development and a major migration from legacy claims systems to a modern policy administration platform. Alongside my full-time work, I am pursuing a Master of Science in Computer Science with a specialization in Human-Computer Interaction at Western Governors University, which I began in July 2026.

   Outside of work, I am developing Pyramids, a gamified productivity iOS app built in Flutter and Dart that turns tasks into a roguelike dungeon. I also co-coached three VEX robotics teams at Sagemont Preparatory Lower School from November 2025 to March 2026, helping advance all three teams to the Florida State Championship Tournamentand earning Mentor of the Year. These experiences reflect my passion for mentorship and for building creative, hands-on technology.

   My academic journey at FIU includes projects like a machine learning stock predictor, a stock analytics dashboard, and a cancer treatment outcome prediction tool — all bridging the gap between theory and impactful, real-world solutions. Above all, I am passionate about building meaningful, human-centered technology that improves lives. Whether working in diverse teams, mentoring others, or pushing myself to grow, I am committed to making a positive, lasting contribution in the tech industry and beyond.
