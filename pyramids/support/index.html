Frequently asked

How do focus battles work?

Tap a task to start a battle, pick a focus length (from 5 up to 60 minutes), and set your phone face down. While you stay present you chip away at the enemy; if you leave the app, it heals. Win the battle and the task becomes a brick in your pyramid.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your tasks and progress stay on your device — no accounts, no ads, no tracking. A small slice of your game progress (coins, streaks, unlocks, and gems) can back up to your own iCloud so a new phone keeps your pyramid, and purchases are handled by Apple and RevenueCat — none of it reaches us. See the Privacy Policy for details.

What is Pyramids Plus?

An optional subscription that adds unlimited task slots, exclusive cosmetics, two arcane relics, and the Deep History insights ledger. Everything you could do before stays free — Plus only adds. It comes with a free trial and can be managed or cancelled anytime in your iPhone's Settings → your name → Subscriptions.

I bought Plus or gems on another device — how do I get them here?

Open the app's Settings and tap Restore Purchases, or open the shop and restore from the paywall. Your subscription and gem balance are tied to your Apple ID, so they follow you to any device signed in with the same account.

What is the optional AI task breakdown?

An off-by-default feature that can suggest how to split a big task into smaller steps. It only runs if you turn it on and add your own Anthropic API key, which stays on your device.

How do I reset my data?

Open Settings in the app to clear app data, or delete and reinstall the app to start fresh. Clearing app data also wipes the iCloud backup of your progress — but your purchased gems are always preserved.
