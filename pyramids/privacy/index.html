Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

The short version

Pyramids is built to respect your privacy.

  • We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data.
  • There are no ads, no analytics or tracking SDKs, and no user accounts.
  • Your tasks, progress, and settings are stored only on your device.

What's stored on your device

The app keeps your tasks, completed-task history ("bricks"), level, coins, streaks, shop items, and settings locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to us. If you delete the app, this data is removed with it.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device (daily nudges, streak reminders, and mindfulness prompts). These are generated on-device and are not sent through our servers.

Optional AI task breakdown

The app includes an optional, off-by-default feature that suggests how to break a task into smaller steps. If — and only if — you turn it on and provide your own Anthropic API key:

  • The text of the task you choose to break down is sent to Anthropic's API to generate suggested steps.
  • Your API key is stored securely on your device and is used to call Anthropic directly; usage is billed to your own Anthropic account.
  • We do not operate a server in this flow, do not receive your task text, and do not store it. Anthropic's handling of that request is governed by Anthropic's own privacy policy.

You can disable this feature and remove your key at any time in Settings.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "last updated" date.
